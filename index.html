About

Hello World Hack is a 12 hour, beginner-orientated hackathon. If you've never attended a hackathon, or recently picked up programming and want to get a project off the ground, this is the hackathon for you!



Filled with workshops on setting up a project, building it, and testing it, awesome prizes and food galore! The best part, it's all free!

FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention event where teams work together to create new products and develop ideas. There are workshops which will explore different technologies and challenges with prizes that teams can work towards. Join a challenge or create something completely your own!

Where/When

The hackathon will be held on Saturday 1st April from 9am to 9pm in the Informatics Forum. This does not include the short opening and closing ceremonies, whose details are soon to be announced.

Food

We will provide 2 meals (lunch and dinner) with snacks, refreshments and drinks to keep you going through the day. Like everything else, it's all completely free!

Who may attend?

Any university student - no matter how little experience you have! HWH is a beginner orientated hackathon and this will be considered when selecting attendees.

How are you beginner-friendly?

Like most hackathons we will be running workshops but many of these will be more basic to cater for those with little experience. Each team will also be assigned a mentor to help them throughout the event.

Teams

You will be working in teams of up to 4 people. You can either come with a team (or part of a team) or we will match you up with others on the day.
